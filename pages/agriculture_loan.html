  Combine Harvestor Loan

Purchase of combine harvesters along with accessories.




  Eligibility

    • Minimum acreage: 6 acres irrigated (dry land 15 acres).
    • Finance available for the approved models of combined harvester.



  Features

    • Type of Facility : Agriculture Term Loan
    • Margin:25% of project cost excluding insurance and registration charges.
    • Interest Rate: 3.50% above one year MCLR.
    • Repayment:Negotiated Repayment. Repayable in 9 yearly or 18 half yearly instalments including grace/gestation period.
    • Processing Charges : 1.40% of the loan amount + GST
    • Insurance: Comprehensive insurance of combine harvester alongwith accessories.



  Security

    • Primary: Hypothecation of combine harvester and accessories.
    • Collateral: Mortgage of land /charge on land or liquid collaterals.



  Documents Required

    • Duly filled in application form
    • Identity proof- Voter ID card/PAN card/Passport/ Aadhaar card/Driving License etc.
    • Address proof: Voter ID card/Passport/Aadhaar card/Driving license etc.
    • Proof of agricultural land/ cultivation.
